About Wicked Good Cupcakes

Welcome to Wicked Good Cupcakes, where our mom-and-daughter team, Tracey and Dani, crafts gourmet cupcakes with premium ingredients, delivered nationwide. As seen on Shark Tank, our unique cupcakes in jars are the perfect solution for anyone searching for a cake shop near me or a memorable gift.

Our cupcakes are handcrafted with rich vanilla beans, velvety chocolate, and creamy buttercream frosting. Each 8 oz jar holds two moist cupcakes with unique fillings like caramel, raspberry, or peanut butter, making them ideal for birthdays, holidays, or corporate events.

Since our Shark Tank debut in 2013, we've become a trusted name for gourmet cupcake gifts. Explore flavors like Chocolate Ganache, Red Velvet, Lemon Zest, or seasonal specials like Pumpkin Spice, and personalize your order with branded labels for a wicked good experience.

Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a sweet indulgence, our cupcake jars make dessert time effortless and fun. We ship directly to your door with care, ensuring every bite tastes as fresh as the day it was baked. Our cupcakes are made in small batches, without preservatives, and packaged in reusable jars—making them an eco-friendly and delicious choice.

Looking for something truly unique? Try our custom cupcake packs where you can mix and match flavors to suit any taste, or send a thoughtful dessert gift complete with a personalized message. Perfect for anniversaries, weddings, graduation parties, thank-you gifts, and corporate giveaways.

From decadent chocolate layers to refreshing fruit-filled creations, Wicked Good Cupcakes offers something for everyone. Order online today and let us help you create sweet memories—one jar at a time.

Our nationwide cupcake delivery ensures that no matter where your loved ones are, they can enjoy the same freshly baked taste you would get in our kitchen. With secure packaging and quick shipping, you can surprise someone special with a jar of happiness—even at the last minute!

Planning a big event? We work with businesses, event planners, and individuals to provide bulk cupcake orders with custom branding, perfect for trade shows, corporate parties, and client appreciation gifts. We also offer seasonal collections, limited-edition flavors, and themed cupcake jars for holidays like Valentine’s Day, Halloween, Christmas, and more.

Every jar is layered with love, ensuring you taste the perfect balance of cake, frosting, and filling in every spoonful. It’s not just dessert—it’s an experience worth savoring.
